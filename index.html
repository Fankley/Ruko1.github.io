Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ruko (Shenzhen Ruko Innovation Technology Co., Ltd.) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Ruko GIM mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Ruko GIM, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address and username for account registration
  • Device model and system version
  • Flight data such as GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, flight logs, and device status (when you manually upload for support)
  • Map location data (if location permission is granted)

2. Permissions We Request

To ensure proper functioning of the App, we may request the following permissions:

  • Location: To display your real-time location on the map and enhance flight control accuracy
  • Camera and Storage: To store photos and videos taken by your drone
  • Microphone: For video/audio recording if enabled
  • Network Access: For firmware updates, map loading, and account login

These permissions are optional but disabling them may affect your experience.

3. Third-Party Services

This App integrates third-party map services, including:

  • Google Maps SDK: Used in regions outside Mainland China. Google may collect device and location data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Amap SDK (Gaode Map): Used in Mainland China. Amap may collect location data. See Amap Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide flight control and live preview services
  • To improve product performance and provide technical support
  • To analyze flight logs (if provided) for troubleshooting
  • To send important notifications about product updates

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless:

  • Required by law or legal authorities
  • You provide explicit consent
  • It is necessary to deliver after-sales service you requested

6. Data Security

We adopt industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Your account and personal data are encrypted and securely stored.
